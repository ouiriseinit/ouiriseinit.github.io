Founding Consultant & Coach

Nicole Washington

Nicole is adept at both the “art and science” of leadership. With over 20 years of corporate experience in the retail industry, her executive background includes pivotal roles in HR and Finance, working directly with Boards of Directors and C-Suite leaders.

She holds an Executive MBA in Strategy & Leadership from UNC Kenan-Flagler and is a certified Change Management Professional and Career Coach. Nicole serves on the Board of Directors for the McColl Center of Art & Innovation and is dedicated to building strong partnerships that catalyze change.
